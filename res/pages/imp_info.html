Preperatory Courses of B.Tech Programmes

All Certificates, Marks Sheets & Other Documents in Original with two sets of Photocopies and scanned copy (in PDF format) of originals in a pendrive in a single folder :

• All certificates (Pass Certificate, if any and Marks Sheets/ Grade Cards) for 10th Class and 12th Class
• All certificates (Pass Certificate, if any and Marks Sheets/ Grade Cards) for 10th Class and 12th Class
• Original Admit Card of JEE(Advanced)2017 and Downloaded Score Card of JEE(Advanced)2017
• Birth Certificate (for Date of Birth)
• Provisional Seat Allotment Letter
• Document Verification-cum-Seat Acceptance Letter issued at Reporting Center of JoSAA-2017
• In case of OBC(NCL) category students, OBC-Non Creamy Layer Certificate in prescribed format given in JoSAA Portal issued on 01.04.2016 or after
• In case of SC/ST category students, Caste Certificate in prescribed format given in JoSAA Portal
• In case of PwD category students, Physical Disability Certificate in prescribed format given in JoSAA Portal
• For all Students: Medical Certificate on your Physical Fitness in prescribed format given in JoSAA Portal
• Original proof of payment of Seat Acceptance Fee Proof (Rs.45,000/- or Rs.20,000/-)
• If your family annual income is less than 5 lakhs in last financial year then Family Annual Income Certificate for the financial year of April 1, 2016 to March 31, 2017 issued by the competent authority (as per Central/State Government Rules)
• Balance amount of fee, if any, to pay through Debit/ Credit Card in POS on the Day of Registration at IIT Guwahati
• Your Color Passport Size and Stamp Size Photographs with white background (at least 16 numbers each), with soft copy in jpeg/jpg format with photo size in between 5 KB to 100 KB.
• If you have Aadhaar Card, then e-Aadhaar Copy (PDF file) and a Photo Copy
• Soft copy of singature as jpeg or jpg format only with size between 2KB to 100KB. of Your Aadhaar Card
